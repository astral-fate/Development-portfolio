Monsha'at 2023 Report

The dataset, sourced from Monsha'at (Saudi Arabia's Small and Medium Enterprises General Authority) through their Open Data Platform, provides a comprehensive overview of business establishments across Saudi Arabia during the first quarter of 2023 (January-March). The data categorizes establishments both by their size (ranging from Micro to Large enterprises) according to the country's Establishment Classification Standard, and by their economic activity using Saudi Arabia's official National Classification of Economic Activities (ISIC). This comprehensive dataset encompasses all geographic regions of Saudi Arabia and includes businesses of all sizes, from very small micro enterprises to large corporations.
